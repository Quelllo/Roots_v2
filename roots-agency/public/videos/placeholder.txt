# Video Placeholders

This directory should contain the following videos:

## Required Videos:

### Hero Section
- hero-bg.mp4 - Background video for hero section
  - Recommended: Abstract motion graphics, creative workspace, or design-related footage
  - Duration: 10-30 seconds (looped)
  - Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
  - Format: MP4 (H.264)
  - File size: Keep under 5MB for performance

## Sources for Videos:
- Pexels.com (free stock videos)
- Mixkit.co (free video assets)
- Coverr.co (free homepage videos)

## Alternative:
If you don't have a video yet, the hero section will work with just the poster image.
Consider using abstract animations, creativity-themed videos, or gradient animations.
